    • Traditional Chicken Afritada


    Ingredients

    • 3 tbsp canola oil
    • 1 clove garlic, minced
    • 1 medium onion, finely chopped
    • 1 red ripe tomatoes, chopped or sliced
    • 3 tbsp soy sauce
    • 300g chicken thigh cut into medium pieces
    • 1 medium carrot, chopped
    • 1 medium potato, quartered
    • 1 green peppers (bell peppers), chopped
    • 0.50 cup (8 fl oz) water
    • 0.50 tsp, ground sage, ground
    • 3 leaves, basil, finely chopped

    Directions

  • 1.) Saut√© garlic and onions; add tomatoes, and soy sauce and reduce to a thick sauce.
  • 2.) In a separate pan, sear the chicken until the skin has some nice brown color. Add the chicken to the tomato sauce and simmer for 2 minutes in low heat.
  • 3.) Add water, carrots, potatoes, and bell pepper. Cover and cooked for 5 minutes or until the stock is reduced to a thick sauce.
  • 4.) Add spices and serve.
