Cooking Time

  • Minutes to Prepare: 5
  • Minutes to Cook: 2
  • Number of Servings: 1

  • Servings Per Recipe: 1
  • Amount Per Serving
  • Calories: 143.4

  • Dietary Fiber: 1.9 g
  • Protein: 2.1 g

    • Faux Bananas Foster


    Ingredients

    • 1/2 medium banana
    • 1/2 tbsp of honey
    • dash of cinnamon
    • 1/4 cup of frozen yogurt

    Directions

  • 1.) The recipe with 1/2 banana makes an individual serving.
  • 2.) Put 1/2 banana into a bowl and pour honey on top. Sprinkle the cinnamon and cook the banana+honey+cinnamon in the microwave for about 1 minute or until soft (if you are doing 2 servings, you will need about 1 minute and 1/2).
  • 3.) The water from the banana will make a watery syrup with the honey. Add the frozen yogurt on top of the warm banana+syrup and enjoy.
  • 4.) This is delicious comfort food, for just 150 calories. And it takes less than 3 minutes - including prep work, cooking, and serving.
